About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at IMT Institute for Advanced Studies Lucca (Italy). My research interests are in the field of convex optimization, and my focus is currently on splitting methods for nonsmooth composite problems.

I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Florence, where I graduated in 2011, with a thesis on numerical methods for Hamiltonian initial value problems, under the supervision of Prof. Luigi Brugnano.

More details about me can be found in my curriculum vitae.
