Curriculum Vitæ

lorenzo.stella@imtlucca.it

I am a Ph.D. student at IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca (Italy) and KU Leuven (Belgium), where I work under the supervision of Panagiotis Patrinos and Alberto Bemporad. My research interests are in the field of nonsmooth optimization.

I obtained my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Florence, where I graduated in 2011 under the supervision of Luigi Brugnano.
