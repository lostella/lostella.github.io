About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at IMT Institute for Advanced Studies Lucca (Italy). My research field is convex optimization, and my focus is now on splitting methods for nonsmooth composite problems.

I obtained my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Florence, where I graduated cum laude in 2011 under the supervision of Prof. Luigi Brugnano.

More details about me can be found in my curriculum vitae.
