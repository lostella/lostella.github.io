Lorenzo Stella
==============

I am a Ph.D. student at `IMT School for Advanced Studies Lucca`_ (Italy) and `KU
Leuven`_ (Belgium), where I work under the supervision of `Panagiotis Patrinos`_
and `Alberto Bemporad`_. My research interests are in the field of nonsmooth
optimization.

I obtained my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Florence,
where I graduated in 2011 under the supervision of `Luigi Brugnano`_.

More in my `curriculum vitae`_. Otherwise you can `contact me`_.

**Software projects** (more in my `Github profile`_)

★ `ForBES`_. Generic and efficient MATLAB solver for nonsmooth optimization
problems. The solver is provided with a library of mathematical functions used
in applications, along with their gradient and proximal operations.

★ `libForBES`_. Framework in C++ for modeling and solving large-scale nonsmooth
optimization problems. It will allow to interface many high-level languages
(including MATLAB, R, Python, Julia) to a unique solver capable of addressing
nonsmooth optimization problems from several application fields.
